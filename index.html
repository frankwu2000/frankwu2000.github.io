A study of Thread user review

CS416 Narrative Visualization

Thread is a new social media platform developed by Meta (a.k.a Facebook). Since its launched in July 5, 2023, millions of user has been using this app to post text, images and videos. People is calling Thread "Twitter killer" as it is the fastest-growing consumer software application in history. It has gained over 100 million users in 5 days.

Through out the rapid user growth, the Thread app has also received large amount of user review on major smart phone platform like IOS store and Google Play store, this narrative visualization intends to show the statistc of these review on the Thread app.

In this narrative visualization, you will see that the positive review of an app is slow declining as the time goes, and the platform of the app will greatly effect user experience.
