A study of Thread user review

CS416 Narrative Visualization

Thread is the latest social media platform application developed by Meta (a.k.a Facebook). Since its launched in July 5, 2023, hundreds of millions of user has been using this app to post text, images and videos. People is calling Thread "Twitter killer" as it is the fastest-growing consumer software application in history. It has gained over 100 million users in 5 days.

Through out the rapid user growth, the Thread app received large amount of user rating on major smart phone platform like IOS App store and Google Play store, this narrative visualization intends to show the trend of these rating on the Thread app.

In this narrative visualization, the app is getting most of rating in the first three days of launching and then declining as the time goes, and different platform of the same app effect user experience and got different review rating distribution. Most people give their rating only using the app for a few days, showing people don't really take time to use the app before giving rating.
