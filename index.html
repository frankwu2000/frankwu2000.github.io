A study of Thread app review

CS416 Narrative Visualization

Thread is a new social media platform developed by Meta (a.k.a Facebook). Since it is launched in July 5, 2023, millions of user has been using this app to post countless text, images and videos. People is calling Thread "Twitter killer" as it is the fastest-growing consumer software application in history. It has gained over 100 million users in 5 days.

Through out the rapid user growth, the Thread app has also received great volume of user review on major smart phone platform like IOS store and Google Play store, this narrative visualization intends to show the statistc of these review on the Thread app.
